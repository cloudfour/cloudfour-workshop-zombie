Welcome to Zombie Preparedness 101

The zombie apocalypse is already upon us. Some have chosen to ignore the signs. Or believe that the few friendly zombies that they and their neighbors know are indicative of the larger zombie populace. Naive.

Thankfully you’ve chosen to get ready for the inevitable. To get the tools to fight back.

You’re in the right place.

What You Will Learn Today

  • Mobile web performance and semantic markup
  • Responsive web design & Mobile First RWD
  • AppCache and GeoLocation
  • jQuery Mobile
  • PhoneGap
  • Device Detection

But before we begin…

The Zombies are coming—make sure to lay in supplies

  • Get ye to your rucksack. You can download your zombie-rucksack at http://cloudfour.github.com/rucksack.zip.
  • Make sure to have a text editor easily at hand.
  • If you have access to a web server (locally or on a hosting provider), that's super. You can simply view example files in your browser, but there are a few exercises you won't be able to participate in.

Inside of your rucksack

You've got to fight zombies. We're giving you some tools.

  • In this pocket you'll find the very-vital field-notes.html. You'll want to open that up first thing in a browser so you can follow along. Go do that now!
  • We know how frustrating it is to get a bit behind in an example during a group exercise. So we've put lots of finished code snippets in here. They're linked to from your field notes.
  • The other pockets in your rucksack (i-got-one, sigma-stronghold-website, etc.) are starting points for the stuff we're going to do.

Act as if your life depends on it…

  • Ask questions.
  • Speak up if you are confused!
  • Give feedback!
